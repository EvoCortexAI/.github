# EvoCortexAI

**Private intelligence for local and edge AI systems.**

EvoCortexAI builds **Saturn**, a private AI control plane that enables individuals and teams to run, approve, route, and audit AI workflows across their own devices and private infrastructure — without sending sensitive data to third-party clouds.

### Core Products

| Product                    | Description                                      | Focus |
|---------------------------|--------------------------------------------------|-------|
| **Saturn One**            | Apple-native private AI workspace                | User interface & workflows |
| **Saturn-Control**        | Swift/Vapor orchestration and policy layer       | Control plane |
| **Saturn-Node**           | Private execution nodes for local inference      | Compute |
| **EvoIntelligenceFabric** | Distributed intelligence and routing layer       | Architecture |

### Our Approach

- **Local-first by default** — Intelligence runs where your data lives.
- **User-governed** — Sensitive actions require explicit approval.
- **Auditable** — Full visibility into models, data access, and execution context.
- **Hardware-aware** — Optimized for Apple Silicon today, designed to support heterogeneous local AI hardware over time.

We believe the future of AI is not just more powerful models, but **better control** over where intelligence runs and how it is governed.

### Current Status

Active development is focused on stabilizing the core control plane and reference implementations. Most repositories remain private while we validate architecture and workflows.
